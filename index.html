Our App

The idea behind Cookoff

At Cookoff, our aim is to to help people eat fewer ready meals and takeaways and instead cook more balanced and nutritional food at home. Our app does this by allowing you to challenge your friends to cook a meal using a special ingredient. After you've all finished cooking, you can share your creations, either directly in the app, or to eat together in real life! Who can come up with the most creative dish?
